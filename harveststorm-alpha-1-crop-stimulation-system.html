Welcome to the Electric Fertilizer Store!

by David Wechsler
published on January 31, 2020

The HarvestStorm Alpha-1 Grow Stimulator is a system designed to make trying out electroculture easy.  Electroculture is an experimental crop-enhancement technology that has been around for over 200 years.  While traditional systems involved the use of large copper plates or high-voltages, this device makes electroculture safe & easy - plug and play!  Based on our research and the needs of plants, some experimentation may be needed.  The system supports 4 different built-in signaling protocols that include well-known frequency groups like Schumann resonance frequencies, Solfreggio frequencies, and others.  The unit is expandable enabling you to share the signal with many other plants or plant groups for protocol-specific efficacy testing or deploying at-scale.  The unit comes with a pair of food-safe electrodes and a UL-rated power supply.  Custom frequencies & protocols are available on-request.
